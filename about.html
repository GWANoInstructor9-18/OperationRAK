Operation Random Acts of Kindness is a game of good deeds! Each act that you complete will put a smile on someone's face. Completed acts quickly and unlock fun new challenges.
HOW TO PLAY: Each day you will be given 5 Random Acts of Kindness to complete. No pressure but...the clock is ticking! Your tasks change after 24 hours. Complete all five tasks in 24 hours to unlock *BONUS tasks. Pick a co-worker, family member, friend, or be bold and unleash your kindness on a perfect stranger!
KINDNESS POINTS: Complete challenges and unlock new levels as you rack up KP's! Earn more KP's for more challenging Acts Of Kindness!
