Operation Random Acts of Kindness is a community of individuals actively seeking to make the world a better place. Join us by playing ORAK!
How to Play: Each day you will be given 5 Random Acts of Kindness to complete. No pressure but...the clock is ticking! Pick a co-worker, family member, friend, or be a super sport and pick a stranger, and begin unleashing the kindness!
Kindness Points: Earn KP’s each time you show some love by completing a challenge. The longer you play and the faster you go, the more KP’s you will earn!
